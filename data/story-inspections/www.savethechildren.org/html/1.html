We work in the hardest-to-reach places, where it’s toughest to be a child.

When you give to Save the Children, 85% of every dollar goes straight to our mission to provide lifesaving relief to children. 

  • $50 can provide enough food to keep 3 9 children from going hungry for a month
  • $150 can wrap warm, cozy blankets around 30 90 children affected by conflict
  • $300 can provide 150 450 face masks to refugee health workers on the front lines

With your support, we can continue our work to keep children in the U.S. and around the world healthy, educated and safe.

As the world celebrates International Day of the Girl this month, we are proud to partner with the Astrid Lindgren Company on the Pippi of Today campaign – which raises awareness for girls on the move and their right to be heard. In celebrating Pippi’s strength, resilience and positivity, we recognize that all girls deserve the chance to take control of their own story- becoming the Pippi’s of today!
